The Snowbird Legacy

Established at the summit of the Wasatch Range, 2001

Our Foundation

The foundation of our organization traces back to the summits of Snowbird, Utah, where a vision of excellence was first established during a historic gathering of minds. It was here, amidst the rugged beauty of the Wasatch Range, that the core principles of innovation and collaborative integrity were codified.

Over the following decades, we have evolved from a small collective of dedicated practitioners into a global standard-bearer. Our history is marked by a relentless pursuit of quality and a commitment to transparency that remains unchanged since the first ink hit the parchment at the original summit meeting.
